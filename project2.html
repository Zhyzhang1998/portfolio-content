CONNECT & QUARANTINE

This is a project focused on informal settlement reformation during the pandemic of COVID-19. The infectivity of the Covid19 virus is closely related to changes in temperature and humidity. The higher the humidity, the weaker the infectivity of the virus, and vice versa. The higher the temperature, the weaker the infectivity of the virus, and vice versa. In shanghai, most flourishing city in China, there are still many informal settlements distributed in the city, remaining being ignored. In order to improve the resistance to infectious diseases in informal settlement, we established a landscape to ensure the social distance and connect the community more tightly.

This is my masterplan, it applied different strategies, like creating activities Space from roof and fragmented space, add more sanitary fixture to achieve less gathering, upper road to split the residents and adjust microclimate humid & hot environment. Apart from that, there are also some small community gardens for residents to do some activities. These strategies are generated after several times of site investigate and interviews of local residents. The data got from the site provided a firm support for our design. And I believe all this design and strategies may split the people flow and decrease the spread of COVID-19.

This is about the Past, Present, and Future of Landscape architecture. Thank you to everyone who has influenced me
