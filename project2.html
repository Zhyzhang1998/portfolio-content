CONNECT & QUARANTINE

This is a project focused on informal settlement reformation during the pandemic of COVID-19. In shanghai, most flourishing city in China, there are still many informal settlements distributed in the city, remaining being ignored.In order to improve the resistance to infectious diseases in informal settlement, we established a landscape to ensure the social distance and connect the community more tightly.

This is my masterplan, it applied different strategies, like creating activities Space from roof and fragmented space and add more sanitary fixture to achieve less gathering.
